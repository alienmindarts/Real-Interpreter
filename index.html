Frequently Asked Questions

What is Real Interpreter?

Real Interpreter is a real-time human interpretation service designed to help non-native speakers communicate clearly in real-life situations across Portugal. You can access the service instantly when needed — no scheduling required.

  • Live interpretation via phone
  • Focused on Portuguese ↔ English
  • For banks, hospitals, public services, etc.
How does it work?

Purchase a plan through our website. When you need help, open the app and call us — an interpreter will join the conversation and translate both sides live.

  • Buy minutes in advance
  • Call via the app or WhatsApp
  • Interpreter speaks with both sides
What plans are available?

We offer three simple Plans. Choose the best one according to your needs:

  • Standard: €10 for 30 minutes (valid 1 year)
  • Basic: €5 for 10 minutes (valid 1 month)
  • Pro: €20/month for 30 monthly minutes
  • Payments processed via Stripe
How can I see my remaining minutes?

Log in to access your personal dashboard. There, you can view:

  • Remaining minutes
  • Plan type and expiration
  • Purchase history
Do I need to install an app?

Not exactly. Real Interpreter is a web app — it runs in your browser. You can add it to your home screen for a more app-like experience.

  • No download required
  • Works on Chrome and Safari
  • Add to home screen in 10 seconds
Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use trusted providers to ensure your privacy and safety.

  • Google Authentication (we never store passwords)
  • Stripe handles payments securely
  • No sensitive personal data is stored
